About

The Fashion & Music Club at the College of Westchester is a creative space where students and alumni can showcase their talents in fashion, music, art, and photography. Our goal is to build a strong community that supports creativity, collaboration, and innovation.

Our Events

🎤 Music Listening Parties

Discover new artists, share playlists, and break down albums together.

📸 Fashion Photoshoots

Create looks, style models, and build your creative portfolio.

💃 Live Dance Performances

Featuring local talent and collaborations with CW’s own dancers.

🎧 Beat & Music Production Workshops

Learn beginner-friendly tools for creating tracks and mixing.
